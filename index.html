MAJOR LEAGUE BASEBALL TEAMS

Baseball is one of America's longest active and most popular sports. Major League Baseball, as an organization, has existed since the early twentieth century and was a pioneer in the commercialization and organization of professional sports in the United States. Once the most popular sport in America, Baseball has remained the third most popular since the popularity of American Football and Basketball skyrocketing in the latter half of the 20th century. Over the history of the MLB, several teams have been established, dissolved and changed. The establishment of additional leagues and the monopoly MLB holds in professional baseball in the United States has solidified its presence in the American and world markets.
